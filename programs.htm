UG COURSES (AIDED PROGRAMMES)

BA - Sociology

The entire syllabus covers the syllabus for civil service examinations & other academic competitive exams.
The course provides valuable insights into social factors and the degree will be an outstanding launch pad for entering the world of business industry and organization.
Syllabus deals with contemporary social phenomena and social problems in India and other countries and creates awareness on gender sensitization and develop critical thinking on various socially relevant issues in our society
Designed to develop the technical skills of social research, provide managerial & computer related knowledge.


Requirements : Pass in +2.
